About

Built by a finance professional.
Not a software vendor.

With over a decade in corporate finance, Mike Tryba most recently served as Senior Accounting Manager before moving into Finance Process Automation -- not to leave accounting behind, but to fix it.

"Most finance tools are built by people who have never closed a month-end. I have, more times than I can count -- and now I build the tools that make it faster, smarter, and less painful."

Mike leads the digital transformation of financial operations, building automated workflows and intelligent document processing systems that turn manual, high-effort processes into reliable engines that run in the background.

His advantage is simple: he speaks fluent accounting and fluent technology. The workflows he builds don't just look good in a demo -- they solve the actual friction that finance teams face at 11pm before a close deadline.

Based in Walpole, MA. Lifelong golfer. Your call gets answered.
